Comprehensive Waterproofing Services

End-to-end waterproofing and civil repair for every structure — backed by expert craftsmanship and material warranty.

🏠Terrace Waterproofing

APP/SBS modified bituminous membranes that resist UV radiation, thermal expansion, and heavy rainfall. Slope correction included. 10-year performance warranty.

  • ✓APP/SBS membranes
  • ✓UV-resistant coatings
  • ✓Drain-slope correction
  • ✓10-year warranty
🏗️Basement Waterproofing

Stop basement water ingress permanently using crystalline compounds that penetrate and seal capillaries. Rated for both positive and negative hydrostatic pressure.

  • ✓Crystalline technology
  • ✓Negative pressure rated
  • ✓Chemical injection grouting
  • ✓Drainage layer system
🔧Crack Repair & Grouting

Structural cracks injected with epoxy or polyurethane resins under pressure to restore the original load-bearing capacity and permanently stop water ingress.

  • ✓Epoxy injection
  • ✓PU foam grouting
  • ✓Structural restoration
  • ✓Flexible sealants
🚿Bathroom Waterproofing

Flexible polymer systems handling constant moisture and movement. Covers floors, walls, and joints with a zero seepage guarantee.

  • ✓Flexible polymer membrane
  • ✓Joint treatment
  • ✓Tile-compatible system
  • ✓Zero seepage guarantee
🏊Swimming Pool Waterproofing

Specialist waterproofing for pools with hydrostatic pressure-rated membranes. Compatible with plaster, tiles, and exposed aggregate finishes.

  • ✓Hydrostatic pressure rated
  • ✓Chemical-resistant
  • ✓Compatible with all finishes
  • ✓All pool types
🏢Exterior Wall Treatment

Silicone-based or acrylic elastomeric coatings that bridge hairline cracks, repel water, and protect facades from monsoon and humidity.

  • ✓Elastomeric coatings
  • ✓Crack bridging
  • ✓Anti-efflorescence
  • ✓Long-lasting finish
🔩Construction Joint Sealing

Sealing of cold joints, expansion joints, and construction joints using pre-swelling waterstops and advanced sealant systems.

  • ✓Bentonite waterstops
  • ✓Pre-swelling strips
  • ✓Expansion joint covers
  • ✓Flexible bond breakers
🏭Industrial Flooring

Heavy-duty epoxy and polyurethane floor coatings for industrial settings offering chemical resistance, wear resistance, and easy cleaning.

  • ✓Epoxy coatings
  • ✓Self-leveling systems
  • ✓Anti-slip finish
  • ✓Chemical resistant

Our Journey

18 Years of Protecting Structures

Hari Dhan Waterproofing was founded in 2005 by Mr. Hari Dhan Sharma, a civil engineer with deep expertise in construction chemistry. Starting with a small team in Bengaluru, the company quickly earned a reputation for delivering permanent waterproofing solutions when others had failed.

Today, we employ 50+ skilled professionals including civil engineers, certified applicators, and project managers. We have successfully completed over 1,200 projects ranging from individual homes to large commercial complexes, IT parks, government buildings, and industrial facilities.

Our technical partnership with leading brands like Sika, Dr. Fixit, and BASF ensures we always have access to the most advanced waterproofing technologies available globally.
